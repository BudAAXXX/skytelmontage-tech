How we work on site

Our work is organised so that planning, access at height and documentation follow a clear structure. The client always knows who is responsible for coordination on site and for the final quality check.

  • Project preparation based on the agreed scope, including site access, expected working methods and required documentation.
  • Brief coordination call or on-site meeting before the main work to confirm current conditions and any changes.
  • Execution with focus on safe access, tidy cable routing and mechanically correct assembly of all components.
  • Final check of the work, structured photo documentation and a short summary of open points, if any.
