Team & cooperation conditions

SkyTel Montage operates multi-person teams for telecom build, maintenance and emergency work at height in Germany and across the EU. The crew combines electrical qualification, rope-access skills (PSAgA) and hands-on experience from projects for major operators and general contractors.

Typical team composition

  • Lead technician / team lead: electrical qualification (NV 194/2022 Â§6/Â§7 or equivalent Elektrofachkraft), responsibility for safety (BOZP/DGUV) and quality.
  • Rope-access technician (L2): assembly, cable routing, installation of brackets and anchor points, rescue planning.
  • Rope-access technician / rigger: additional works, finishing, photo documentation and detail checks.

What is billed per team member

  • Day rate: professional work on site, preparation, documentation and use of standard tools.
  • Travel costs: share of van rental, fuel, tolls and parking, allocated according to project and number of technicians.
  • Per diems & accommodation: usually according to German / Czech rates, invoiced as agreed in the contract.
  • Special equipment: optional surcharges for specific measurement or test equipment (e.g. EMF meters, electrical testers, special rescue kits) requested by the project.

Payment options

  • Invoicing via Czech limited company (EU VAT): invoicing in EUR or CZK, standard payment terms.
  • Milestone-based billing: project split into phases such as build, commissioning and final acceptance.
  • Monthly summary invoices: one consolidated invoice per month for all sites and days worked.

Benefits for partners

  • Single point of contact (CEO / team lead) with clear responsibility for planning, safety and delivery.
  • Photo and technical documentation prepared in DE/EN/CZ according to project standards (e.g. W+A, DFMG, operators).
  • Option for rapid deployment on urgent calls (weekends / nights), depending on capacity and contract.
  • Transparent breakdown of costs per team member and per used equipment, suitable for audits and recharging.
