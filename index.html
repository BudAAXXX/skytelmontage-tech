Telecom services in Germany and the EU

SkyTel Montage focuses on construction, modernisation and service of radio and telecom systems for network operators and general contractors. We work on towers, rooftops and special structures from greenfield sites to swap programmes and emergency interventions.

  • Installation of steel structures, antenna systems and RF / microwave links, including cable trays and grounding.
  • Modernisation and swap of existing sites (antennas, RRUs, cabling), integration support and basic measurements.
  • Troubleshooting and corrective maintenance at height, including access planning and rescue concepts (PSAgA).
  • Photo and technical documentation according to German and Czech project standards (DE/EN/CZ).

Team & cooperation conditions

SkyTel Montage deploys multi-person teams for telecom rollout, maintenance and emergency work at height in Germany and across the EU. The crew combines electrical qualification, rope-access skills (PSAgA) and experience from projects for major operators and general contractors.

Typical team composition

  • Lead technician / team lead: electrical qualification (NV 194/2022 ¬ß6/¬ß7 or equivalent Elektrofachkraft), responsible for safety (DGUV) and quality.
  • Rope-access technician (L2): assembly, cable routing, installation of brackets and anchors, rescue planning.
  • Rope-access technician / rigger: additional work, finishing, photo documentation and detailed checks.

What is billed per team member

  • Day rate: professional work on site, preparation, documentation and standard tools.
  • Travel costs: share of van, fuel, tolls and parking, allocated according to project and crew size.
  • Per diems & accommodation: usually according to German / Czech rates, invoiced as agreed.
  • Special equipment: optional surcharges for specific measurement and test equipment (e.g. EMF meters, electrical testers, special rescue kits) requested by the project.

Benefits for partners

  • Single point of contact (CEO / team lead) with clear responsibility for planning, safety and delivery.
  • Photo and technical documentation in DE/EN/CZ according to project standards (e.g. W+A, DFMG, operators).
  • Option for rapid deployment on urgent calls (weekends / nights), depending on capacity and contract.
  • Transparent breakdown of costs per team member and equipment, suitable for audits and recharging.
