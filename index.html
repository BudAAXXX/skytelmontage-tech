How we work on site

The crew is organised so that planning, execution at height and documentation go through one responsible person. This simplifies communication for the client and reduces the risk of misunderstandings between different subcontractors.

  • Clear scope confirmation before deployment, including access conditions, rigging concept and expected documentation.
  • Daily on-site risk assessment, toolbox talk and coordination with the client or site manager.
  • Clean execution with focus on cable management, corrosion protection and correct torque of mechanical joints.
  • Final visual check, photo documentation and a concise report highlighting any open points.
